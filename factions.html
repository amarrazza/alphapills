Factions

~ Choose wisely ~

The Alchemix faction are a lending-and-mercenary group that runs a "clones-for-loans" service - anyone can get a loan in exchange for being cloned...Their clone must then pay off their debt: forced to complete jobs both sinister and benign until set free

The Yearn Faction, guided by the four yFu, who interpret the knowledge of the Yearning Tree - an ancient being who hears the desires of creatures across the universe - and responds by growing yield-bearing seeds containing the answers to their wishes

OlympusDAO is known as The Three - a cult whose members seek transcendence by being eaten by The Protocol, bonding to it for all eternity. Cultists who pledge to the "3,3" climb the mountain "seeking $OHM" and an audience with The Protocol. Few ever make it

The Uniswap faction exist to support the Unigod, a deity that exists to maintain the balance of de/centralization. Temples and statues of all sizes are freely constructed to channel the Unigodessence, a liquid used to transmute memes and memories into matter
